Our Promise To You

We will always use the least abrasive materials possible to clean your vessel's hull and running gear. This will keep your bottom paint as safe as possible and extend the time between haul-outs. We will also strive to deliver honest and accurate reports about the condition of your vessel below the waterline. This inclues the condition of your bottom paint, any damage to your hull or running gear, and the life of your zincs. For customers on regular cleaning schedules, zincs will be changed only when necessary to avoid gratuitous costs and corrosion.
